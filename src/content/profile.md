I’m Ali Salem, a Toronto-based multidisciplinary Product Designer and tech enthusiast. Over the past 10 years, I worked at the intersection of experience design, visual design, design strategy, and digital transformation, helping brands deliver engaging digital products across a wide array of industries. I’m always driven by a sense of responsibility towards enabling and empowering others through the power of design and technology.

I’m currently Lead Product Designer at [League](https://league.com), where I lead design for the Genesis Design System that powers League’s digital health platform.

Previously, I was Design Systems Lead at [Auth0](https://auth0.com), where I built scalable foundations for theming, accessibility, and developer tools that streamlined interface development across products.

Before that, I was Visual Design Lead for RIG Design System at [Royal Bank of Canada](https://www.rbcroyalbank.com), supporting 700-plus practitioners in delivering a unified digital banking experience for over 16 million customers.

Earlier in my career, I worked at digital design and innovation agencies in Toronto, Dubai, and Cairo, collaborating with brands like Entercom, Emirates, Etisalat, MasterCard, Jumeirah, and Emaar.

As a systems thinker, I’m passionate about building scalable design systems that enable consistent, high-quality experiences.
